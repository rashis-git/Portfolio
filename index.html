01. About Me

I'm a Product Consultant at IBM by day, building AI products on the side. Over the past 4 years, I've led product rollouts across 72 business units, shipped 3 AI web apps from scratch, and improved a GenAI tool's accuracy from 50% to 75% by rethinking the product strategy.

At IBM, I work on Oracle Cloud implementations—configuring expense policies, building procurement integrations, and solving regional compliance challenges like China's Fapiao system. It's enterprise software, but the problems are real: cross-functional coordination, stakeholder management, and making complex systems work for actual users.

Outside work, I build AI tools. DayFlow is a productivity app I designed and shipped end-to-end. I also built an Oracle RAG chatbot to help consultants navigate 1000+ pages of documentation, and an ATS resume optimizer that I actually use for my own job applications.

I like working at the intersection of product thinking and technical implementation—figuring out what to build, then actually building it.
