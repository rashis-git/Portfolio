⚠️ The Challenge

75% of resumes never reach human eyes due to ATS filtering. Job seekers struggle to match keywords while maintaining a natural, human tone.

💡 The Solution

A Multi-LLM Resume Optimizer that extracts job-specific keywords, restructures content using STAR method, and filters 75+ AI buzzwords for authenticity.

🔥 Pain Points
  • ✕
    ATS systems reject resumes without exact keyword matches
  • ⏳
    Manual keyword extraction is time-consuming & error-prone
  • ⚙
    AI-generated content sounds robotic and inauthentic
🛠️ Solution Architecture
01
Resume Upload — User submits resume + job description
02
Keyword Extraction — LLM identifies critical job keywords
03
STAR Restructure — Content rewritten with STAR method
04
Tone Filter — 75+ buzzwords filtered for human voice
