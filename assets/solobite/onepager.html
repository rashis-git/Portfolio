SoloBite

AI-Powered Meal Companion for Solo Diners

Prototype Ready FoodTech
⚠️ The Challenge
  • 35% of urban Indian households are single-person
  • Every recipe app defaults to 4 servings, wastes food
  • Solo diners waste 30–40% more food proportionally
  • Decision fatigue at 8 PM = Swiggy wins
💡 The Solution
  • One smart input: text, photo, or voice — AI figures out intent
  • Single-portion, nutritionally-complete recipes from what you already have
  • Tiered pantry intelligence (always-have vs usually-have)
  • Cross-ingredient weekly plans to minimize waste
🔥 Pain Points
  • ✕
    Recipes default to 4 servings; scaling breaks ratios
  • ⏳
    No app connects fridge contents to macro targets
  • ⚙
    “What to cook” paralysis leads to food delivery
🛠️ Solution Architecture
01
Smart Input — text, photo upload, or voice capture
02
AI Recipe Engine — Gemini 2.0 Flash via OpenRouter
03
Nutrition Engine — TDEE-based macro targets per meal
04
Delta Suggestions — “Add paneer (+₹40) for +12g protein”
